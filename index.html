About the Event

Instaseed-The 10 Minutes Funding is an opppurtunity for budding entreppreneurs to pitch their startup ideas and kick start itself as an accomplished venture.

Each startup will get 10 minutes to present their breakthrough business concepts to a panel of investors and incubators who then decide wether they want to invest in any of the entreprises or not.

To reward their talent and apprecieate their ingenuity,we wish to provide them with seed funding,incubation assistance and experiential mentoring which could help young entrepreneurs in establishing their own startup,aiding them in conceptualizing and executing their plans by fine-tuning their strategy and bring them on platform where they can interact with industry and hence further hone their enterprising skills.
